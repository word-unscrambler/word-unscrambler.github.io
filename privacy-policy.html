Privacy
Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Collect

Word Unscrambler is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personal information. The only data we store is your theme preference (light/dark mode) which is saved locally in your browser using localStorage.

How We Use Information

Any text you enter into our tools is processed entirely in your browser. We do not send your searches to any server or store them anywhere. Your data stays on your device.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. We may use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences, but these do not track your browsing activity across websites.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to display typography on our website. Google may collect some anonymous usage data as described in their privacy policy. We do not use any other third-party analytics or tracking services.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to secure. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your word searches and puzzles remain private.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our contact page.
